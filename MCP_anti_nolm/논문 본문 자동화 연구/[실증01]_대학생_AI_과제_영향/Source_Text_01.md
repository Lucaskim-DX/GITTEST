# Research Summary: Spinal Disorders Anatomy and Causes

### 1. Definition and Classification
Spinal disorders refer to any abnormality, deformity, or injury to the spine, which acts as the body's central pillar.
- **Degenerative**: Herniated Intervertebral Disc, Spinal Stenosis, Spondylosis.
- **Structural**: Scoliosis, Kyphosis, Spondylolisthesis.
- **Traumatic**: Compression fractures.

### 2. Common Causes
- **Aging**: Dehydration of discs and thickening of ligaments.
- **Posture**: Chronic stress from poor occupational habits.
- **Weakness**: Lack of core muscle strength (Soft tissue instability).

### 3. Anatomical Locations
- **Lumber (L4-S1)**: Highest incidence due to load-bearing.
- **Cervical (C5-C7)**: High mobility area leading to mechanical wear.
